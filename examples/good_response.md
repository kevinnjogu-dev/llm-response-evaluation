# Good Response Example

## Prompt

Explain the importance of regular exercise.

## AI Response

Regular exercise is important because it improves physical health, supports mental wellbeing, and helps maintain overall quality of life.

Physical benefits include improved cardiovascular health, stronger muscles and bones, and better energy levels. Exercise can also support mental health by reducing stress, improving mood, and enhancing cognitive function.

A balanced exercise routine should include activities that improve strength, flexibility, and endurance while considering an individual's health goals and abilities.
